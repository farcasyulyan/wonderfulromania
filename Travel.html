Danube Delta

The Danube Delta is so beautiful that you never get tired of admiring it. And it is so different, with each passing season, that an area you see in the summer will seem completely changed in the spring or late fall. That's why you can never say you really know her. Letea Forest fascinates you at any time, and Sfantu Gheorghe's arm leaves you speechless with amazement in any season. And it's as if the food tastes different at the end of summer.

Poiana Brasov

- Poiana Brasov is an oasis of peace for all categories of tourists in all seasons. In summer, the mountain-learned tourist can go hiking in the Postavarul massif and its surroundings, discovering the numerous marked routes, but he can also capture the true nature, entering the paths walked only by wildlife. In winter, the ski slopes are available to tourists of varying degrees of difficulty for both beginners and advanced skiers. 		reclame

Following events

Navy day-15.08.2022

On this occasion, demonstrations take place both in Constanţa and in the other sea and river port cities. The magnitude of the Navy Day holiday has increased year by year, the festivities consisting of: cannon salutes, raising the big peacock, the word of the Navy Commander or his representative, singing the national anthem, throwing a flower anchor in the water, in memory of sailor heroes, sailor sports games , boat competitions or fireworks. With Romania's accession to the North Atlantic Alliance, the Romanian Navy Day is honored by the presence of foreign allied or partner ships, which actively participate in this holiday.

Untold 04-07.08.2022

David Guetta, Don Diablo, G-Eazy, Kygo, Major Lazer, Morten, Oliver Heldens, Paul Kalkbrenner LIVE and Tujamo are part of the seventh story from the magical realm created for 4 days and 4 nights in Cluj-Napoca. They are joined by Above & Beyond and the British artist Anne-Marie, a premiere for our country. One of the most appreciated and awaited artists, Kygo , is preparing a special show for UNTOLD. The Norwegian DJ and producer is the one who laid the foundations of a new style in electronic music, tropical house, to which he added his favorite instrument, the piano.

Rockstadt 03-07.08.2022

„Rockstadt Extreme Fest” made its debut in 2013 and takes place annually in August in Rasnov, close to the fortified citadel. It was designated the best festival organized in Romania in 2013 and promises an intense experience in the heart of nature. „Rockstadt Extreme Fest” is the only festival dedicated to extreme metal in our country. In the perimeter of the festival there is an amusement park – Adventure Metal Park, which has a zip line, an elastic trampoline, a climbing wall and archery equipment.
